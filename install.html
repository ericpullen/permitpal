Add Permit Pal to your phone

Install it straight from your browser — no app store. It opens full screen and works offline, just like a regular app.

iPhone & iPad (Safari)

  1. Open Permit Pal in Safari (this only works in Safari, not Chrome or other browsers).
  2. Tap the Share button at the bottom of the screen.
  3. Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen.
  4. Tap Add in the top corner. The Permit Pal icon appears on your home screen.

Android (Chrome)

  1. Open Permit Pal in Chrome.
  2. Tap the ⋮ menu in the top-right corner.
  3. Tap Add to Home screen (or Install app).
  4. Tap Add. The Permit Pal icon appears on your home screen.

✅ Now open Permit Pal from your home screen like any app — full screen, and ready even with no signal.
